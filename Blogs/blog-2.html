Reflections on My First
3 Months with RBC

Rotation 1: Operations for Commercial Banking - Build your communication, teamwork and customer relationship skills by supporting clients in the Commercial Service Advisor role

Getting ready to work in a hybrid format I feel...

I’m excited for the change to hybrid work! As much as I like the comfort of home, I feel like I need the face-to-face time with my colleagues to really connect and get to know them properly. For me, is the perfect solution to get a balance between the pros and cons of work-from-home and working in-office. It’s been really great so far being able to get to know everyone better and see their faces!

Key takeaways from the RBC Personal Effectiveness Series are...

When I first heard the term ‘Radical Candor’ I was highly skeptical, but after the session I would really like to get the book and read it for myself. The in-depth discussion on what it means to find the balance between being authentic yet remaining sensitive to other people's feelings was deeply insightful. These things seem trivial at a glance, yet they are absolutely pertinent to forming good relationships not only with our coworkers but in general. The point on how it’s a moral obligation to be candid to those you care for really changed my perspective. Usually, I would let people be the way they are without wanting to intervene or get involved as a way to respect their privacy and space. However now I see the value of speaking out and the ways you can do it respectfully and would like to try it more in my day-to-day life.

After the operations training, I felt...

I felt much more informed after completing the training! I majored in economics at university where I did many courses on banking and finance, but it doesn’t prepare you for all the realities of what goes on behind the scenes. It’s been eye opening to compare the textbook learning to the realities of real-world banking as well as being a part of it.

The support I received was...

I have received so much support from RBC during my entire time with the company I am really blown away by the overall uplifting and positive company culture. I worked at a few companies before joining the Career Launch Program and from my experience it is rare to see such a positive work environment that is truly committed to fostering growth of its employees. I'm trying my best to pay it forward if there’s any way I can help out others around the team also!

Looking forward to the rest of the Career Launch Program!
